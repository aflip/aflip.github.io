### [Explore IDSP](https://github.com/aflip/IDSP)

IDSP is a decentralized laboratory-based IT-enabled disease surveillance program to monitor and respond  epidemic prone diseases. Data is collected and reported on epidemic prone diseases on weekly basis. The weekly data reports are uploaded on [IDSP's website](https://idsp.mohfw.gov.in/index4.php?lang=1&level=0&linkid=406&lid=3689) as PDFs. In the repo I have a script that extracts the data from the PDFs and creates a usable dataset.
